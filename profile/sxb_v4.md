# SHoNgBot V4 Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 10, 2026

SHoNgBot V4 (“SHoNgBot”, “the Bot”, “the Application”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a Discord bot used for community management, tickets, temporary voice rooms, subscriptions, activity systems, rewards, staff tools, moderation support, and server automation.

This Privacy Policy explains what Discord-related data SHoNgBot V4 processes, why it is processed, where it may be stored, and how users can contact us regarding privacy-related requests.

This policy applies only to SHoNgBot V4 and its related systems. It does not replace the privacy policies of Discord, Ko-fi, Patreon, or any other third-party services used with the Bot.

---

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to SHoNgBot V4 and its related services, including:

- Discord bot features
- Ticket systems
- Temporary voice-room systems
- Economy, XP, rewards, streaks, and activity systems
- Subscription and supporter features
- Staff management tools
- Moderation and administrative systems
- Databases and cache systems
- Logs and monitoring tools
- External services used to operate bot features

By using SHoNgBot V4 or interacting with its features in a Discord server, you acknowledge that limited Discord-related data may be processed as described in this policy.

---

## 2. Data We Collect and Process

Depending on the features used, SHoNgBot V4 may collect, receive, store, or process the following categories of data.

### Discord account and server data

- Discord User IDs
- Guild IDs
- Channel IDs
- Message IDs
- Role IDs
- Usernames
- Display names
- Avatar URLs
- Server membership status
- Role and permission information
- Member-related data needed to verify access to bot features

---

### Ticket data

When users open or interact with tickets, the Bot may process and store:

- Ticket type
- Ticket reason
- Ticket owner
- Assigned staff member
- Ticket priority
- Open, claim, and close timestamps
- Ticket status
- Ticket channel ID
- Administrative notes
- Ticket transcripts, where enabled
- Messages and content included in a ticket transcript

Ticket transcripts may be retained for support, moderation review, dispute resolution, abuse prevention, and administrative auditing.

---

### Temporary voice-room data

For temporary voice-room features, the Bot may process:

- Room owner ID
- Room name
- Room channel ID
- Member limit
- Privacy settings
- Trusted users
- Blocked users
- Temporary access codes
- Voice duration
- Related activity statistics
- Message counts associated with supported room systems

This data is used to create, manage, protect, and customize temporary voice rooms.

---

### Activity, XP, economy, and reward data

The Bot may process data related to activity and rewards, including:

- XP points
- Coins or currency
- Transfer records
- Daily streaks
- Game rewards
- Eligible message counts
- Voice activity duration
- Leaderboard data
- Reward history
- Economy-related logs

This data is used to operate activity systems, rewards, rankings, economy features, and anti-abuse controls.

---

### Subscription and supporter data

If Ko-fi, Patreon, or supporter-related features are enabled, the Bot may process:

- Subscription status
- Subscription tier
- Expiry date
- Grace period status
- Pause or suspension status
- Ko-fi-linked email address
- Ko-fi transaction ID
- Patreon member ID
- Supporter role configuration
- Custom theme settings
- Custom role settings
- Custom emoji settings
- Custom keyword settings
- Supporter-only feature settings

SHoNgBot V4 does not collect full payment-card details. Payment processing is handled by external providers such as Ko-fi or Patreon. The Bot only processes the data needed to verify and manage supporter features.

---

### Staff and administrative data

For staff management and administrative workflows, the Bot may process:

- Staff shift activity
- Staff message counts
- Staff voice duration
- Ticket actions
- Leave records
- Notes
- Warnings
- Timeouts
- Bans
- Moderation reasons
- Administrative action logs
- Staff performance or activity summaries

This data is used for internal administration, operational review, moderation accountability, and community management.

---

### User-provided content

The Bot may process content submitted by users through bot-managed systems, including:

- Ticket reasons
- Ticket messages and transcripts
- Application or form responses
- Administrative notes
- Auto-response settings
- Role names
- Color values
- Custom keywords
- Custom configuration values
- Any text submitted through commands, forms, buttons, modals, or bot-managed workflows

The Bot does not intend to collect Discord passwords, authentication tokens, full payment-card information, or private financial information.

---

### Technical and operational data

The Bot may process technical data needed for operation and security, including:

- Error logs
- API logs
- Rate-limit data
- Temporary cache keys
- Anti-spam indicators
- Abuse-prevention signals
- System diagnostics
- Feature usage logs

This data is used to keep the Bot stable, secure, and resistant to misuse.

## 3. How We Use Data

SHoNgBot V4 uses data only as necessary to operate its features and protect the community.

Data may be used to:

- Run bot commands and interactions
- Create and manage tickets
- Generate and store ticket transcripts
- Create and manage temporary voice rooms
- Apply room privacy and permission settings
- Calculate XP, coins, rewards, streaks, and leaderboard positions
- Manage economy and transfer features
- Manage clans, teams, roles, and related channels
- Verify Ko-fi and Patreon subscriptions
- Assign and remove supporter roles and benefits
- Enable supporter features such as custom roles, emojis, themes, keywords, and auto-responses
- Track staff activity, shifts, leave, warnings, and administrative actions
- Prevent spam, abuse, fraud, and economy manipulation
- Send notifications or direct messages related to tickets, subscriptions, rewards, or feature status
- Diagnose errors and improve Bot stability
- Support moderation and administrative review

We do not sell Discord user data.

We do not use Discord user data for advertising or unrelated marketing.

## 4. Discord Permissions and Privileged Intents

SHoNgBot V4 may require Discord permissions and Gateway Intents to operate its features.

Depending on enabled features, the Bot may need to:

- Read messages in permitted channels
- Manage messages
- Create channels
- Manage channels
- Manage roles
- Manage members
- Send direct messages
- Create threads
- Apply timeouts
- Ban users
- Process interactions
- Read member and role information

The Bot uses these permissions only to perform configured features, enforce server rules, or execute authorized administrative actions.

---

### Server Members Intent

SHoNgBot V4 may use member data to:

- Verify whether a user is still in the server
- Check user roles and permissions
- Assign or remove roles
- Manage subscription-based roles
- Apply staff or administrative permissions
- Control access to ticket, room, supporter, and moderation features
- Maintain temporary voice-room permissions
- Support anti-abuse and moderation workflows

---

### Message Content Intent

SHoNgBot V4 may process message content only where required for enabled features.

Examples may include:

- Ticket messages and transcripts
- Prefix commands, if enabled
- Auto-response or keyword-based features
- Staff or moderation notes
- Feature configuration entered by users
- Message-based reward or activity systems, if enabled
- Anti-spam or abuse-prevention features, if enabled
- Bot-managed workflows that require reading user-provided text

The Bot does not use Message Content to sell data, advertise to users, or train AI models.

Where possible, features may use Discord interactions, slash commands, buttons, modals, or context menus instead of reading ordinary message content.

## 6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the Bot, manage the community, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, and maintain administrative records.

Retention may vary depending on the type of data:

- Redis cache data, rate-limit data, cooldowns, and temporary states are usually short-lived and may expire automatically.
- Ticket data and transcripts may be retained for support, administrative review, moderation history, and dispute resolution.
- Subscription data may be retained while required to verify supporter status, grant benefits, and manage subscription history.
- XP, economy, streak, reward, and leaderboard data may be retained to preserve user progress and system integrity.
- Warnings, bans, timeouts, notes, staff actions, and moderation records may be retained longer to protect the community and support administrative review.
- Error logs and technical logs may be retained according to system configuration and operational needs.

Some data may be deleted or anonymized when it is no longer needed or when a valid request is approved, unless retention is required for security, moderation, administrative, legal, or anti-abuse reasons.

## 9. Security

SHoNgBot V4 uses databases, cache systems, and hosting infrastructure to operate its services.

Sensitive credentials may include:

- Discord bot tokens
- Database credentials
- Redis passwords
- Supabase keys
- Ko-fi or Patreon API credentials
- Lurkr API credentials
- Sentry DSN or monitoring credentials
- Hosting credentials

These credentials are intended to be stored in environment variables, deployment configuration, secret managers, or protected server settings.

They should not be published in public source-code repositories.

Access to stored data should be limited to authorized owners, developers, administrators, or staff who require access for operational, moderation, support, or security purposes.

Although reasonable safeguards are used, no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Users should avoid sharing passwords, authentication codes, private financial information, or other highly sensitive personal information through Discord or bot-managed channels.
